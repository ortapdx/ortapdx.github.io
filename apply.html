ORTA Membership Application

You can make a membership application now online.
Please review below information carefully before proceeding.
  • You must reside in the State of Oregon, or Washington State's Clark or Skamania Counties.
  • You need a credit card (VISA, MC, AmEx, DISCOVER) in your name with the billing address within above locations.
  • We will verify your name and address for ORTA membership records by having PayPal charge your credit card just ONE CENT.
  • Please make sure you choose the option to donate with a credit card, and select "Share your mailing address" option at the next screen.
  • Once donation is completed successfully, you will be redirected to fill a membership information form to finish the application.



(*) Don't have a credit card? Reach out to us at apply@ortapdx.org to learn more on alternate ways to apply.
